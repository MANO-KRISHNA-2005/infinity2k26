ABOUT US

Infinity, a dynamic platform for innovation and excellence organized by the B.Sc Computer Systems & Design students of the Applied Mathematics & Computational Sciences Department since 2019. We feature diverse, student-curated events that transform concepts into groundbreaking solutions. Infinity bridges classroom learning, celebrating creativity, collaboration, and technical brilliance. With every edition, it inspires participants to refine their skills and unleash their potential. Join us to experience the journey of Beyond the Binary...
